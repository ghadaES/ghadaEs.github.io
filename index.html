Vincent Van Gogh

Vincent Van Gogh was a Dutch post-impressionist painter, considered one of the world's most famous and influential figures of Western art. He is best known for his paintings, which amounted to about 2000 artworks in just over a decade, of which about 800 were oil paintings, created in the last two years of his life. He committed suicide at the age of 37 due to severe psychological disorders and poverty.
